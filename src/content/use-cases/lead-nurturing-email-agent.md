## Overview

An email agent that detects new leads, automatically responds instantly with 3 slots in your calendar and awaits a response. It handles any scheduling suggestions, then confirms and books the meeting.

## Workflow & Automation

- New lead received via email
- Respond with potential meeting slots
- Receive response from lead and confirm slots

## Benefits

- Save time
- Always on 24/7 responses
- Increased focus as not trying to schedule manually

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: LLM text to text

**Automation Tools**: Zapier

**Integrations**: Google Mail, Google Calendar
