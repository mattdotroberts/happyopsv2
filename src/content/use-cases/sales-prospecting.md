## Overview

Generic cold emails have low response rates, making outreach inefficient. AI automates personalised email outreach based on prospect behavior and interests.

## Workflow & Automation

- AI scrapes LinkedIn and company websites for prospect insights
- AI generates personalised email content based on the prospect's industry, interests, and role
- Emails are sent at optimal times based on AI-predicted engagement

## Benefits

- Improves email open and response rates
- Reduces manual email personalisation efforts
- Ensures timely follow-ups with prospects

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: NLP-based email personalization, engagement prediction

**Automation Tools**: Apollo.io, Outreach.io, Reply.io

**Integrations**: Gmail, LinkedIn, HubSpot CRM
