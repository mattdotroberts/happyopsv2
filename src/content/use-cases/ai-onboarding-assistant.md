## Overview

New hires often need immediate answers to common questions outside of HR working hours. AI-powered chatbot provides 24/7 personalised support for new employee queries.

## Workflow & Automation

- Natural language processing of employee questions
- Contextual responses based on role and department
- Automated escalation of complex queries to HR

## Benefits

- Provides instant 24/7 support
- Reduces HR support ticket volume by 80%
- Improves new hire satisfaction

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: LLMs OpenAI GPT-4, Claude 3.5 Sonnet

**Automation Tools**: Intercom, MobileMonkey, ManyChat

**Integrations**: Slack, MS Teams, Knowledge bases
