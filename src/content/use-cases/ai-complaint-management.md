## Overview

Managing employee complaints manually is time-consuming and can lead to inconsistent handling. AI-powered complaint management ensures every issue is properly categorized, routed, and tracked.

## Workflow & Automation

- Employee submits complaint through portal or email
- AI analyzes content to determine category and severity
- Complaint is automatically routed to appropriate handler
- Follow-up reminders and escalations are automated

## Benefits

- Ensures consistent complaint handling
- Reduces response time by 70%
- Creates audit trail for compliance
- Identifies patterns and systemic issues

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: Sentiment analysis, text classification, priority scoring

**Automation Tools**: ServiceNow, Zendesk, custom workflows

**Integrations**: HR case management systems, email, Slack
