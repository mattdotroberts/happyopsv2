## Overview

Coordinating multiple onboarding activities and stakeholders manually is complex and time-consuming. AI automates the scheduling of all onboarding activities while optimising for stakeholder availability.

## Workflow & Automation

- AI analyses calendar availability across departments
- Automatic scheduling of orientation, training, and team meetings
- Smart prioritisation of critical onboarding activities

## Benefits

- Reduces scheduling conflicts by 95%
- Optimizes stakeholder time and availability
- Ensures consistent onboarding schedule execution

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: OpenAI GPT-4 Agents, Claude Sonnet

**Automation Tools**: Calendly, Microsoft Bookings, ServiceNow

**Integrations**: Calendar systems, Team collaboration tools
