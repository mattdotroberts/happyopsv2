## Overview

HR teams spend countless hours manually processing documents like resumes, contracts, and employee forms. AI-powered document processing automates extraction, classification, and data entry.

## Workflow & Automation

- Documents are uploaded or received via email
- AI extracts relevant information using OCR and NLP
- Data is automatically categorized and entered into HR systems
- Exceptions are flagged for human review

## Benefits

- Reduces document processing time by 90%
- Eliminates manual data entry errors
- Ensures consistent document handling
- Frees HR staff for higher-value work

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: OCR (Optical Character Recognition), NLP for entity extraction

**Automation Tools**: DocuSign, Adobe Acrobat AI, UiPath

**Integrations**: HRIS systems, document management platforms, email
