## Overview

Hiring managers spend too much time manually reviewing resumes, leading to delays and potential bias in candidate selection. AI-powered resume screening to automatically shortlist candidates based on predefined criteria.

## Workflow & Automation

- Job applications are submitted through an online portal
- AI scans and categorises resumes based on skills, experience, and keywords
- Candidates meeting the criteria are ranked and forwarded to hiring managers

## Benefits

- Faster hiring process
- Reduced human bias in candidate selection
- Improved match rates between candidates and job roles

## Key Technologies & Tools

**AI Models**: LLM Resume Tools

**Automation Tools**: n8n, Microsoft Power Automate

**Integrations**: ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems), HR software
